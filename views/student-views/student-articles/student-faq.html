Student FAQs

Quick answers to the most common onboarding, compliance, and dashboard questions students ask as they get ready for clinical placements.

Results update automatically while you type and include related student articles.

Use the “Forgot Password” link on the login page to reset your password. Once you sign back in, go to My Account > My Security Settings to update your two-factor authentication delivery preference (text, authenticator app, etc.).

A red status indicates something is overdue, expired, or missing documentation. Open the requirement, read the instructions carefully, and upload the requested files (vaccination record, lab result, form, etc.). Once submitted, the requirement will move to “Pending Review” until a coordinator approves it.

Check the email address listed in your CPNW demographics. Emails may go to spam/junk, so look there as well. If you still cannot find the message, contact your program coordinator to have the link reissued. Keep in mind that the Checkr link expires after 7 days.

Schedules are released by your program or healthcare partner once all compliance requirements are cleared. Watch the Dashboard notifications and emails from your coordinator for the official “ready” status and any next steps.

Start with your program coordinator—they can advise on documentation requirements and escalate to CPNW support when needed. You can also email support@cpnw.org with your full name, program, and a description of the issue.
